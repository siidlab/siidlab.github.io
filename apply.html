SIID Lab is seeking exceptional researchers and students to work on Human Computer Interaction and 3D User Interface Research. Our research agenda encompasses: 1) AI-powered intelligent user interfaces for AR/VR/XR applications; 2) Novel interaction techniques for AR/VR/XR applications; 3) Remote collaboration in immersive environments; 4) Advancing high-fidelity XR prototyping through generative AI

1. Student Researchers

‍Start Period: Summer 2025

Responsibilities:
- Develop AR/VR/MR prototypes for user studies
- Perform research-related tasks, including user study, research paper writing
- Attend weekly progress meeting

Required Qualifications:
- Undergraduate or Graduate students majoring in Visualization, Computer Science, Information Science, or other related fields
- Previous research experience or publications in HCI/3DUI venues preferred
- Strong programming skills and practical experience in Unity AR/VR development
- Experience with AR/ML libraries and back-end development is a plus Self-motivated with the ability to take initiative on projects

‍2. How to apply

‍Please submit the following materials to Dr. Jong-In Lee ( jongin@tamu.edu):
- CV
- Online portfolio and/or code repository
- (optional) 2-3 Representative publications (pdf) relevant to the research
